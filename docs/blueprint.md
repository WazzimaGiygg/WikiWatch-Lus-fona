## Core Features:

- Violation Catalog: Displays reported violations with details on the accused, violation type, and description.
- Timeline Visualization: Presents a chronological view of key events related to abuse and violations.
- Legal Information Display: Provides information on relevant legal aspects such as LGPD and Marco Civil da Internet.
- Action Plan Guidance: Offers guidance on how to take action, including reporting to relevant bodies.
- Email Template Generation: Generates and pre-fills email templates for reporting violations to Privacy Team and Ombuds Commission, serving as a tool for users.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep red (#B91C1C) to convey urgency and importance.
- Background color: Dark gray (#1E293B) to provide contrast and focus.
- Accent color: Purple (#8B5CF6) to highlight interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' for a clean and modern reading experience.
- Use Phosphor Icons for a consistent and modern look.
- Use glass panel components with backdrop blur for a modern, translucent UI.
- Subtle fade-in animations when switching between tabs or loading content.
